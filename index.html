About Kumono

What is DeFi?

Decentralized Finance(DeFi) is a blockchain-based form of finance that does not rely on central financial intermediaries such as brokerages, exchanges, or banks to offer traditional financial instruments. The DeFi crypto market cap is over $110,000,000,000 as at today. Kumono Finance aims to contribute to the DeFi ecosystem with NFT rewards features

What is Kumono?

Kumono was founded in 2021 with the intention of being a truly decentralized finance project. Kumono is a protocol designed to help promote popular cryptocurrency concepts to a wider audience. It introduces holders to cutting-edge concepts such as participation rewards, non-fungible tokens(NFTs). decentralized exchanges, and more. It is launched on the Binance Smart Chain(BEP-20) allowing for fast, efficient trading. Where it makes tradeoffs - decentralization, censorship resistance and security are prioritized. There is a 2% tax on both buying and selling . This tax will be collected and distributed to the top 20 holders of the $KUMO governance token. $KUMO will be the official governance token for the Kumono ecosystem and will be used for governance for any future development on the Kumono platform
